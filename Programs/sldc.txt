Why SDLC is Important?
Provides structure and discipline in software development
Helps in planning, cost estimation, and resource allocation
Improves quality and customer satisfaction
Ensures timely delivery of the final product

Popular SDLC Models:
Waterfall Model (linear and sequential)
Agile Model (iterative and flexible)
V-Model (testing runs in parallel with development)
Spiral Model (focuses on risk analysis)
Iterative Model (repeated cycles of development)
Big Bang Model (little to no planning, mostly used for small projects)
